Final Project



Automatic atmosphere sensitive DJ application

There will be a total of 3 projects, but I only know what the final project will be about. My idea is an automatic DJ, that plays tunes that go well with the setting of a hangout, party, lounge, or whatever event requiring ambient music.

The application is projected to extract data from many different measurements that it will use to make a choice. Some examples could be:

- Temperature
- User desires (genre, type of party etc.)
- Weather
- Movement measured from Webcam
- Microphone to determine loudness and mood
- Time of day
- And many more factors that may influence the human music experience
